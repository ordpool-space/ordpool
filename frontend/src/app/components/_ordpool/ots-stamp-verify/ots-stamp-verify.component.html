Stamp & Verify

Drop a file here to stamp it with three public OpenTimestamps calendars (alice, bob, finney), or drop a .ots receipt to verify one. Everything happens in your browser. Stamping sends only the file's SHA-256 hash, never the file itself; we don't operate the calendars and we never see your file.
